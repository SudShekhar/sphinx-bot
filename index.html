Welcome to Sphinx.

Sphinx is the easiest way to keep a track and improve your english vocabulary. We lay special emphasis on CAT, GMAT and GRE preparation and many of our questions will be very useful to participants preparing in the above exams.

Benefits

  • Huge question bank: We've 1000+ questions already and are adding more on a daily basis
  • Adaptive testing: The difficulty of questions adjusts with your performance.
  • Daily Reviews: You can sign up for our daily review letter which will contain useful information such as the words you got wrong, your performance charts, comparision with others etc.

Privacy Policy

  • We don't use your information anywhere.
  • We won't send you any spam, ads or unwanted messages. Use the bot to learn, stop when you feel like
  • Rating the page/sharing with friends is completely optional. You won't be bothered to do so.
